About Me

Everyone has dreams, and I do have my dreams too. However, before I reach them, there is a certain path I need to take. My past experiences have strengthened me to the point that I can adapt quickly to any environment, should it be expected of me.

 

Work Experience

I've worked in different positions and various professional areas, including an internship at a High Court and an Embassy. I've also had more humble jobs: receptionist in a Hotel or computer lab assistant in College.

Personal Development

My personal growth has been fueled by my multicultural background and the many international experiences I've went through. During the past 7 years, I have lived in 5 different countries on 3 different continents.

Languages

I speak 4 languages fluently and I am learning 3 more. I can work in English, česky, Deutsch and tiếng Việt, and I have advanced knowledge of Français and 普通話.
